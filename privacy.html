Data Collection and Usage

Google Sign-In (Firebase Authentication)

Purpose of Processing: To securely link your "Pro" status to your account, this app offers a sign-in option via a Google account. This service is provided by "Firebase Authentication," a service of Google Ireland Limited. This allows you to use unlocked features across multiple devices. The processing of data by Google Firebase is based on a Data Processing Agreement in accordance with Art. 28 GDPR. We do not receive access to your Google password or other login credentials; these are managed solely by Google.

Data Collected: When you sign in with Google, we receive access to the data required to create your user account, primarily your email address and a unique user ID (Firebase UID). While Google may also provide your public profile name, we do not actively use this name within our app.

Device-Specific Customizations

To improve the user experience, the app reads the device manufacturer upon first launch. This is done for the sole purpose of providing users of specific devices (e.g., Xiaomi) a more specific and understandable guide for configuring the necessary system settings. The manufacturer's name is not stored or transmitted to us.

Data Storage & Transfer: Your email address is stored within Firebase's Authentication system to uniquely identify your account. In our application database (Google Cloud Firestore), we only store your pseudonymized user ID along with your Pro status. Data processed by Google may also be transferred to countries outside the EU, such as the USA. Google uses the EU Commission's Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) to ensure an adequate level of data protection for such transfers.

Processing of Transaction Data for Purchase Management: To reliably manage in-app purchases (e.g., "Pro" status) and process refunds correctly, we store a pseudonymized transaction token (Purchase Token) provided by Google in your user profile within our database after a successful purchase. In the event of a refund via the Google Play Store, Google informs us of this event. An automated process on our server (a Cloud Function) uses the stored token to associate the refund with your account and revoke the corresponding Pro features. This processing serves the purpose of contract fulfillment and the correct management of your license rights.

Legal Basis: The processing of this data is for the fulfillment of a contract (provision of Pro features) in accordance with Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR.

Data Processing by Third Parties (Google Play Billing)

For purchasing paid content, we use the Google Play Billing service. When you make a purchase, the necessary data for processing is handled directly by Google. We do not receive access to your sensitive payment data. For more information, please see Google's Privacy Policy.

Contact via Email

When you contact us via email, the data you provide (e.g., email address, name, content of the message) will only be processed for the purpose of handling your request. This data will not be shared without your consent and will be deleted once the request has been fully processed, unless legal retention obligations apply.

Crash Reports and Diagnostic Data (Google Play)

To improve the stability and reliability of our app, we use the integrated crash reporting service provided by the Google Play Console (Android Vitals). If the app crashes, anonymized information is sent to Google and made available to us. This data includes, for example, the device type, operating system version, and the stack trace of the crash. This information does not contain any personally identifiable information and is used solely for the purpose of identifying and fixing bugs. The legal basis for this processing is our legitimate interest in maintaining a functional and stable application in accordance with Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR.

Data Retention

We store personal data only as long as necessary for the fulfillment of the stated purposes or as required by law. Data stored in Firebase Authentication is retained until you delete your account. When you delete your account, all personal data in Firebase Authentication will be permanently and irrevocably deleted. If you wish to request the deletion of your personal data, you can contact us at any time using the details provided in the "Data Controller" section. Alternatively, you can find detailed instructions on our Account & Data Deletion page. We will delete your data promptly unless legal retention obligations prevent this.

Data Stored Locally on Your Device

To ensure the app's functionality, certain settings (Filter Settings, Routine Settings, Theme Setting) are stored exclusively on your device using Android's internal SharedPreferences function. This data never leaves your device and is never transferred to our servers or to third parties.

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your data. However, please note that complete security of data transmission over the internet cannot be guaranteed.

Required App Permissions

  • Display over other apps (SYSTEM_ALERT_WINDOW): Required to display the dimmer overlay above other apps.
  • Run at startup (RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED): Needed to reactivate the Routine feature after the device restarts.
  • Ignore battery optimizations (REQUEST_IGNORE_BATTERY_OPTIMIZATIONS): Ensures that scheduled routines can run reliably in the background.
  • Foreground service & Notifications (FOREGROUND_SERVICE & POST_NOTIFICATIONS): Required for stable operation and to display a permanent notification for transparency, as mandated by Android.

Xiaomi) eine spezifische und besser verständliche Anleitung zur Konfiguration der notwendigen Systemeinstellungen anzuzeigen. Der Herstellername wird nicht gespeichert oder an uns übertragen.

Daten können auch in Länder außerhalb der EU (z.B. die USA) übertragen werden. Google sichert dies über Standardvertragsklauseln (SCCs) der EU-Kommission ab.

Verarbeitung von Transaktionsdaten zur Verwaltung von Käufen: Um In-App-Käufe (z.B. den "Pro-Status") zuverlässig zu verwalten und Rückerstattungen korrekt zu verarbeiten, speichern wir nach einem erfolgreichen Kauf einen von Google bereitgestellten, pseudonymisierten Transaktions-Token (Purchase Token) in Ihrem Nutzerprofil in unserer Datenbank. Ein automatisierter Prozess auf unserem Server (eine Cloud Function) nutzt den gespeicherten Token, um die Rückerstattung Ihrem Konto zuzuordnen und die entsprechenden Pro-Funktionen zu widerrufen.
