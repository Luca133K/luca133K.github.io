Data Collection and Usage

Google Sign-In (Firebase Authentication)

Purpose of Processing: To securely link your "Pro" status to your account, this app offers a sign-in option via a Google account. This service is provided by "Firebase Authentication," a service of Google Ireland Limited. This allows you to use unlocked features across multiple devices. Data processing by Google Firebase is based on a Data Processing Agreement (Art. 28 GDPR). We do not receive access to your Google password.

Data Collected: When you sign in with Google, we receive your email address, name, and a unique user ID (Firebase UID).

Data Storage & Transfer: Email and name are stored in Firebase Authentication. In our Firestore database, only the anonymous user ID and Pro status are stored. Data may be transferred outside the EU, e.g., to the USA, under Google's Standard Contractual Clauses.

Legal Basis: Processing is required for contract fulfillment (Pro features) under Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR.

Data Processing by Third Parties (Google Play Billing)

Purchases are handled directly by Google Play Billing. We do not receive your payment data. More info: Google Privacy Policy.

Contact via Email

Data you provide via email (name, email address, message) is processed only to respond to your inquiry and deleted afterward, unless legal retention applies.

Data Retention

We store personal data only as long as necessary or legally required. Firebase Authentication data is deleted when you delete your account. Contact us anytime to request deletion.

Data Stored Locally on Your Device

Settings (Filter, Routine, Theme) are stored only on your device via Android SharedPreferences. These never leave your device.

Data Security

We implement technical and organizational security measures, but cannot guarantee absolute security over the internet.

Required App Permissions

  • Display over other apps: Required for the dimmer overlay.
  • Run at startup: Reactivates routines after device restart.
  • Ignore battery optimizations: Ensures routines run reliably in the background.
  • Foreground service & notifications: Required for stable operation and transparency notification.
