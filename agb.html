3. Integrated Safety Mechanism: The App includes a proactive safety mechanism to minimize the theoretical risk of screen damage (esp. "burn-in" on OLED displays) due to uneven sub-pixel wear from prolonged use of color filters. This mechanism operates as follows:

  • a) Upon continuous, active use of a color filter ("Night Mode", "Red Filter", "Purple Filter"), its intensity is automatically and gradually reduced after staggered periods of time (e.g., after 3, 5, 9, and 12 hours).
  • b) The timer relevant to this mechanism is paused when the device's screen is turned off.
  • c) The timer is completely reset if the device's screen has been turned off for a continuous period of more than four (4) hours.
  • d) The user is informed of a reduction via a brief on-screen message. A manual increase of the intensity by the user after an automatic reduction is possible; however, the underlying timer will continue its countdown.
